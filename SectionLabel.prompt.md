The eyebrow + display title that opens every section — a 13px uppercase tracked label in the accent colour, with an optional large display title below.

```jsx
<SectionLabel title="Design work.">Artwork</SectionLabel>
<SectionLabel title="Through the lens.">Photography</SectionLabel>
```

- Label is always UPPERCASE with `0.12em` tracking; titles are sentence-case and end with a period (brand voice).
- Omit `title` to use just the eyebrow.
