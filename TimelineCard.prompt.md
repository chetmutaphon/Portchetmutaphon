A single experience entry for the timeline — soft-grey card with a role/period header row, the company in the accent colour, and a grey description. Nudges `+4px` right on hover.

```jsx
<TimelineCard
  role="Marketing Executive"
  company="Company Name"
  period="2023 — Present"
  desc="Led integrated marketing campaigns and brand strategy across digital and print channels."
/>
```

- Designed to sit beside a vertical rail + dot drawn by the parent timeline (see the portfolio UI kit).
- Use em-dashes in `period` (`2023 — Present`) per brand voice.
